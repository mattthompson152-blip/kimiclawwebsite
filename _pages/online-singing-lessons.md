    <h2>Why Online Lessons Work</h2>
    
    <h3>You're More Comfortable at Home</h3>
    <p>Most people sing better when they're relaxed. In your own space, there's no studio intimidation, no travel stress, no waiting rooms. Just you and your voice.</p>
    
    <h3>Better Focus</h3>
    <p>Without the distractions of a physical studio, many students concentrate more on their technique. You're looking at a screen, but you're listening to your body — and that's what matters.</p>
    
    <h3>Recorded for Review</h3>
    <p>Every lesson can be recorded (with your permission). You get a permanent reference to practice with — something that's rarely practical with in-person lessons.</p>

    <h2>What You Need</h2>
    
    <p><strong>Required:</strong> A smartphone, tablet, or computer with a camera. An internet connection. A space where you can sing comfortably.</p>
    
    <p><strong>Recommended:</strong> Headphones or earbuds (reduces echo). Good lighting so I can see your posture.</p>
    
    <p>That's it. No expensive microphones, no professional equipment. The simplicity is the point.</p>

    <h2>How a Lesson Works</h2>
    
    <ol>
      <li><strong>Check-in</strong> (5 min) — How did practice go? Any challenges since last time?</li>
      <li><strong>Warm-up</strong> (10-15 min) — Tailored exercises for your voice that day</li>
      <li><strong>Technique work</strong> (15-20 min) — Specific exercises for your goals</li>
      <li><strong>Repertoire</strong> (15-20 min) — Applying technique to songs you want to sing</li>
      <li><strong>Practice plan</strong> (5 min) — Clear instructions for what to work on next</li>
    </ol>

    <p>Plus: recorded lesson and written practice notes after every session.</p>

    <h2>Platforms I Use</h2>
    
    <p><strong>FaceTime</strong> — Best for Apple users. Great audio quality, no app needed.</p>
    
    <p><strong>WhatsApp</strong> — Works on any smartphone. Lower bandwidth, good for variable connections.</p>
    
    <p><strong>Microsoft Teams</strong> — Join via web browser, no account needed. Best for laptop users.</p>
