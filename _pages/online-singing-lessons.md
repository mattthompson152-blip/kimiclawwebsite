      <h2>What You'll Learn in Online Singing Lessons</h2>
      
      <h3>Core Vocal Techniques</h3>
      
      <p><strong>Breath Support and Control:</strong> Learn the foundation of good singing — how to breathe efficiently, support your tone, and eliminate strain. Proper breath control transforms your voice instantly.</p>
      
      <p><strong>Range Extension:</strong> Expand your high and low notes safely. Whether you want to hit those high notes without cracking or strengthen your low register, I'll show you how.</p>
      
      <p><strong>Register Blending:</strong> Eliminate the "break" between your chest voice and head voice. Learn to move smoothly through your entire range without audible transitions.</p>
      
      <p><strong>Tension Release:</strong> Identify and eliminate the physical tension that holds your voice back. Most singers carry unnecessary strain in their jaw, neck, and shoulders.</p>
      
      <p><strong>Tone and Resonance:</strong> Develop a clear, ringing tone that projects effortlessly. Learn how to use your resonators for maximum sound with minimum effort.</p>
      
      <p><strong>Pitch Accuracy:</strong> Train your ear and your voice to sing in tune consistently. We'll work on interval training and pitch-matching exercises.</p>
