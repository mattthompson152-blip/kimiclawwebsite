      <h3>Complex Techniques, Simple Explanation</h3>
      
      <p>Celebrity vocal training can seem mysterious or exclusive. My "Easy Way" method breaks down advanced techniques into simple, actionable steps:</p>
      
      <p><strong>Instead of:</strong> "Place the voice in the mask with proper resonance"<br>
      <strong>I say:</strong> "Hum and feel the buzz in your lips. That's where your voice lives."</p>
      
      <p><strong>Instead of:</strong> "Support with the diaphragm while maintaining a lifted soft palate"<br>
      <strong>I say:</strong> "Breathe low like you're smelling a flower. Keep that openness when you sing."</p>
      
      <p><strong>Instead of:</strong> "Navigate the passaggio with balanced cord closure"<br>
      <strong>I say:</strong> "Slide smoothly from low to high like a siren. Feel how your voice changes gear."</p>
