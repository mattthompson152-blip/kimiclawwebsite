      <h2>Our Commitment</h2>
      
      <p>At Sing The Easy Way, we believe that great singing shouldn't cost the earth. As an online vocal coaching business operating from a mobile studio, we're committed to minimising our environmental impact while delivering world-class lessons to students worldwide.</p>
    </div>
    
    <div class="card" style="padding: 2rem; margin: 2rem 0;">
      <h2>Carbon-Negative by Design</h2>
      
      <p>Our business model is inherently sustainable:</p>
      
      <ul>
        <li><strong>No Commute:</strong> By teaching online, we eliminate travel emissions for both teacher and students. No driving to studios, no flights for international clients.</li>
        <li><strong>Mobile Studio:</strong> Our motorhome studio operates on solar power and efficient energy systems, minimising our carbon footprint while maintaining professional teaching standards.</li>
        <li><strong>Paperless:</strong> All lesson notes, practice materials, and communications are digital. No printing, no shipping, no waste.</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
    
    <div class="card" style="padding: 2rem; margin: 2rem 0;">
      <h2>Sustainable Operations</h2>
      
      <ul>
        <li><strong>Renewable Energy:</strong> Wherever possible, we use campsites and locations with renewable energy sources.</li>
        <li><strong>Energy Efficiency:</strong> Our mobile studio uses LED lighting, efficient heating, and low-power equipment.</li>
        <li><strong>Minimal Waste:</strong> We practice zero-waste principles in our daily operations, recycling and composting wherever facilities exist.</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
    
    <div class="card" style="padding: 2rem; margin: 2rem 0;">
      <h2>Carbon Offsetting</h2>
      
      <p>While our digital-first approach significantly reduces emissions compared to traditional in-person teaching, we recognise that some carbon impact is unavoidable. We offset our remaining carbon footprint through verified tree-planting and renewable energy projects.</p>
    </div>
    
    <div class="card" style="padding: 2rem; margin: 2rem 0;">
      <h2>Continuous Improvement</h2>
      
      <p>We regularly review and update our environmental practices. Our goal is to operate as sustainably as possible while maintaining the high-quality teaching our students expect.</p>
